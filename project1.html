Wander Ways

Wander your way to a great travel experience!

Project Overview

Wander Ways is a travel app designed to help users explore curated activities by destination and build personalized itineraries. Our team aimed to create an intuitive and accessible interface that caters to travelers of all ages.

My Role

I led user research, app design, and prototyping, ensuring that accessibility and ease of use were prioritized throughout the process.

Key Challenges

  • Information overload from multiple travel resources.
  • Difficulty in creating flexible yet personalized itineraries.
  • Ensuring accessibility for diverse age groups.

Tools Used

Figma, Google Slides, Canva
